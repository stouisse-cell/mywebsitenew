Elaf Sky View

ApartmentsCentre VilleEnergy ReadyHigh-Spec FinishesDaam Sakani EligibleActive Mandate

Located in Centre Ville, in a well-established green neighbourhood beside a public garden, Elaf Sky View places residents about 10 minutes on foot from the TGV station and about 5 minutes by car from the motorway.

Designed for both rental income and long-term living, the project offers investor-friendly opportunities as well as comfortable personal residences. Each unit includes air-conditioning pre-installation, double-insulated windows and glass doors, Turkish-imported doors with triple deadbolt systems, fully equipped kitchens, built-in shower seating, and elevated interior finishes throughout.

Each apartment also benefits from private rooftop space for future solar panels, directly connected to the unit, allowing owners to significantly reduce electricity costs once installed. An underground parking stall is assigned to each apartment and included in the property title.
