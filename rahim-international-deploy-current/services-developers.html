Our Approach

Not a Broker.
A Commercial Partner.

Many middle-market developments in Morocco are built seriously but sold traditionally: limited marketing, informal information, weak investor materials, and sales teams without the tools to explain the full value of the project.

Rahim International sits between business consulting, marketing agency, and sales partner. We help developers clarify the offer, structure the investment case, present the project professionally, and reach buyers who would otherwise never see it.

Partnership Terms

What a Serious
Mandate Requires.

We are most useful when a developer wants more than exposure. A good mandate requires access to information, decision-maker responsiveness, credible pricing, and the willingness to present the project properly.

What We Bring

Business analysis: review of pricing, buyer segments, project strengths, objections, and competitive position.

Investment case architecture: a clear narrative buyers can understand, not only a unit list and floor plans.

Marketing production: collateral, copy, digital assets, campaign direction, and sales material organization.

Buyer distribution: outreach to local, diaspora, and international buyer segments when relevant.

Sales coordination: structured follow-up from enquiry to reservation, notary coordination, and close.

What We Require

Clear mandate scope: agreement on what we are responsible for and what the developer controls.

Legal and technical documents: permits, plans, title information, specifications, delivery schedule, and payment terms.

Developer responsiveness: timely answers, access to decision makers, and reliable follow-up when buyers ask serious questions.

Credible pricing: a price-quality ratio that can stand up to comparison and buyer scrutiny.

Sales alignment: agreed timelines, availability, reservation rules, and coordination with notaries or internal sales staff.
